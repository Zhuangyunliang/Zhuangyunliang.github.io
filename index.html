Biography

​ Chen Lyu graduated with a PhD in Computer Software and Theory from the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2015. He is currently an associate professor of Shandong Normal University School of Information Science and Engineering, a master’s supervisor, a member of the CCF Collaborative Computing Committee, a member of CCF YOCSEF Jinan, and a member of the Shandong Intelligent Science and Technology Committee. Served as a program committee member of ATC, NCSC, CSCW and other academic conferences.

​ Mainly research intelligent software engineering, high-dimensional data mining and interdisciplinary applications. In recent years, he has presided over 6 national and provincial research projects and 6 horizontal projects entrusted by enterprises and institutions, and participated in a number of key scientific and technological projects of the National Natural Science Foundation of China and the State Grid as the sub-project leader or technical backbone.He has published more than 30 related academic papers in well-known international academic conferences including ICSE, IEEE Trans, Journal of Computers, and domestic and foreign journals, and applied for more than 20 national invention patents and registered software copyrights.
